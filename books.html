A DOG WITH ATITUDE

When he was a bitzer, his name was Barney. Rescued from the Pound by four metros, he morphed into Elliott the TV-star glitzer, because although he looked like a very ordinary mutt, one of them recognised this pooch had charisma and put him in a TV ad.

But what didn’t change about Elliott is his quirky take on humans, especially his four Timeshare owners. He understands every word they say, and boy, you should hear his comments! Can dogs sometimes be laughing at us?
