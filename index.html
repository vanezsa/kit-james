ABOUT KIT JAMES

Former city boy Kit James lives and writes in rural Australia. His stories are mostly about dogs and rock’n roll, and the quirky, funny side of life.

Kit’s Dad was probably the UK’s most dedicated rock’n roll fan. He saw them all, right through the Sixties, his rabid fandom leaving an indelible impression on the young Kit. Kit has had a lifelong fascination with ‘charisma’ and what it means to have it.

Kit relocated to an abundant wine region with his Australian wife, ’Kat’, and his dog Kaboodle, wine being his other great passion.
